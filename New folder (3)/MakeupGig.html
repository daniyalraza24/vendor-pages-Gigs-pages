What are the questions to ask when choosing a caterer?

  • Makeup of your choice: Bridal makeup / Party makeup for family/ Engagement makeup/HD makeup/ Airbrush makeup. As we all know that makeup trends keep on changing it is advisable to hire a MUA who can suggest you best for your face.
  • Hairstyling: Just like the wedding makeup, hairstyle can also make or break or look. Be it soft curls for your sangeet or a floral bridal bun for your wedding your MUA knows it best to make you look like a princess on your wedding.
  • Draping: Sari/ Bridal Dupatta: This might be very tiny thing but very handy when your makeup artist or hairstylist is well equipped with draping a sari or bridal dupatta.
  • Nail polish change:You would not want a plain nails on your wedding, right. Be it a neutral color gel or a bright red nail polish many MUA also gives this service.
  • Fake Eyelash Extensions: Your eyes are the main part. Mastering in eye makeup is something that only few bridal makeup artists are good at it. Get a fake eyelash extension, which will make your eyes look bigger and prettier.
  • Outstation makeup charges: Do ask your makeup artist if they will spend their money on the travel allowance or would you have to pay for their travel.

Do makeup artists travel to the venue?

Usually makeup artists do travel to the wedding venue to provide their services to all their clients. Whether the makeup artists are from the same city or is travelling to a completely different city to provide their services, they visit their clients to provide their services. However in some cases, the makeup artists prefer that clients visit their makeup studio to get their makeup done. This is usually the case when they have multiple booking for the day or just prefer to provide the services only at their salon or studio.
